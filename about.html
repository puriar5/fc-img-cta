  • Our Mission

    Together with communities, FoodCorps connects kids to healthy food in school.

  • Our Vision

    We are creating a future in which all our nation’s children—regardless of race, place or class—know what healthy food is, care where it comes from, and eat it every day.

  • Our Work

    FoodCorps advances social health and justice through food by placing service members in schools across the nation; by supporting, influencing, and advocating for legislation and policy; and by providing resources for anyone passionate about bringing positive change to their community.
