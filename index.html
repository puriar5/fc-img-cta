Food can change everything. And school is the place to start.

Our vision: By 2030, every kid in America — regardless of race, place, or class — will have access to hands-on food education and healthy food in schools.

The Case for Food in Schools

Yum! FoodCorps Service Member Rebecca Lee unearths a carrot with a 6th grader at PS 145 in Oakland, CA.

Here’s what you can do

Join our service corps

Make a difference by teaching kids about healthy food and helping schools become healthier places to grow and learn.

Learn more & apply

Bring FoodCorps to your community

FoodCorps’ partners — schools, districts, and community organizations — are ripe for change and value food in and out of the classroom.

Partner with us

Join the movement

Take action in your community and across the nation by joining FoodCorps’ action network. No matter who you are or what you do, you can help change the school food system.

Learn more

Support FoodCorps

Help us continue to work toward healthier futures for all kids.
