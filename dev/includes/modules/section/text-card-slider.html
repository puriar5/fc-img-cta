step 5

5 tips to make your application shine

1

Demonstrate knowledge about the community you want to serve in, or at least a community very similar to it.

2

Show your personality! We are looking for people with compelling personal stories, not cookie-cutter candidates.

3

Tell a story about a specific pivotal moment or experience that inspired you to pursue this path in your personal statement.

4

Upload a video or other supplemental file that expresses your passion for and connection to FoodCorps’ mission and what it means to you — be creative! We also encourage a good, personalized letter of recommendation.

Please note: You are welcome to include links as supplemental files. Just insert the link to your video, writing, or other content in a document you upload.

5

Connect with a FoodCorps staff member before applying in order to learn more about what service is like in your region of choice. Many of our program coordinators and managers are alums of the FoodCorps program. Because they have served with FoodCorps and are now supporting current service members on the ground, they have great insight into the service experience and how it varies from site to site. You can find contact information for the state coordinators, managers, and directors at the top of each state page.
