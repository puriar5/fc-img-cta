Our AmeriCorps program’s investment in next-generation leaders includes:

Nutrition & Public Health

Health educators, researchers, and clinical dieticians are the essential professionals who work to make our society healthier every day. They study, teach, communicate and practice safe and healthy food, sound nutrition, and good dietary habits.

Education

Kids’ formative experiences in school are as critical to their development as what they eat. School teachers and leaders, and educational administrators teach, inspire, and nurture them, and prepare them for thriving lives as adults.

The nonprofit sector drives positive change in our world. It’s full of passionate people who dream of a better, more just society, and work tirelessly to create it in so many ways—from the arts to the streets, from the global environment to local needs.

School Food Leadership

School nutrition directors play the most powerful role in shaping the day-to-day operations of school food. They work with school leaders and food service staff to create sustainable cafeterias that truly nourish kids.

Policy & Advocacy

Policy makers, influencers, and advocates are the voices who articulate and establish the guidelines and laws that guarantee healthy food for all. Policy and advocacy workers lead us, ultimately, toward a more just society.
