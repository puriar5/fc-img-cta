Benefits of service

FoodCorps offers members a unique and transformative experience. You’ll make a measurable difference in the lives of your students and increasing equity in school systems while gaining professional skills that are transferable to many different career paths. And you’ll join a network of passionate, dedicated changemakers that will serve and support you no matter where your journey takes you in the future.
