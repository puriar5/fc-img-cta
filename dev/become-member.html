STEP 3

Where you’ll serve

FoodCorps currently serves schools and districts across the country in 12 states and the District of Columbia. You can choose up to three preferred states, and even specific service sites, in your application. We encourage you to serve in your local community, as we are looking to empower local leaders. Keep in mind that we do not provide housing.
